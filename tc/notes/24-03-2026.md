# Técnicas de Compilação

## LR parsing

Recursive when the grammar is left-recursive, meaning that a non-terminal symbol can be expanded to itself as the leftmost symbol in one of its production rules. For example, if we have a production rule like `A -> A alpha`, where `A` is a non-terminal and `alpha` is a sequence of symbols, this would be left-recursive. Left recursion can lead to infinite recursion in a top-down parser, which is why it is not suitable for LR parsing.
